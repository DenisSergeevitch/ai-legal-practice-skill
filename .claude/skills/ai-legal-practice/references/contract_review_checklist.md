# Contract review checklist (practical)

Use this to spot **missing** or **underpowered** provisions and to keep reviews consistent.

> Notes:
> - “Missing” can also mean “present but too vague / too one-sided.”
> - Always confirm deal type and the party perspective before applying.

---

## Universal (almost every commercial agreement)

- Parties correctly identified (full legal names, entity type, jurisdiction, address)
- Defined terms:
  - consistent capitalization
  - no circular definitions
  - definitions match operational sections
- Term:
  - start date, renewal mechanics, notice periods
  - termination for cause vs convenience
- Payment:
  - fees, invoicing, payment terms, late fees, taxes
  - refunds / credits / setoff rights
- Confidentiality:
  - definition of confidential info
  - exclusions
  - permitted disclosures
  - duration
  - return/destruction
- IP:
  - background IP vs foreground IP
  - ownership of deliverables and “work product”
  - license grants (scope, territory, term, sublicensing)
- Data protection / security (if any personal or customer data involved)
- Warranties and disclaimers
- Limitation of liability:
  - cap
  - exclusions (e.g., confidentiality, IP infringement, willful misconduct)
  - consequential damages waiver
- Indemnification:
  - scope, procedures, defense control, settlement consent
  - IP infringement indemnity (if relevant)
  - third-party claims definition
- Insurance (if relevant)
- Compliance with law (including export controls, sanctions, anti-corruption if relevant)
- Assignment / change of control
- Subcontracting
- Publicity / press releases / use of logos
- Audit rights (if relevant)
- Dispute resolution:
  - governing law, venue
  - arbitration vs court
  - attorneys’ fees
  - injunctive relief carve-out
- Notices (addresses, effective delivery)
- Force majeure
- Relationship:
  - independent contractor
  - no partnership / agency
- Entire agreement, order of precedence
- Amendment / waiver formalities
- Severability, counterparts, electronic signatures

---

## Services / SaaS agreements (vendor/customer)

- Scope of services:
  - SOW structure
  - acceptance criteria, deliverables, timelines
  - change order process
- SLAs:
  - uptime definitions
  - service credits (sole remedy?)
  - exclusions and measurement
- Customer obligations:
  - cooperation, access, data accuracy
- Usage restrictions and acceptable use
- Data:
  - customer data ownership
  - processing purposes
  - retention/deletion on termination
  - security measures + incident response
  - subprocessors
- IP:
  - vendor platform ownership
  - customer feedback license
  - open-source components disclosures
- Compliance:
  - HIPAA/GLBA/PCI etc (only if actually applicable)
- Benchmarking restrictions (sometimes important for vendors)
- Suspension rights for nonpayment / security risk
- Transition assistance / data export on termination

---

## Stock/asset purchase agreements (M&A)

- Definitions: “Material Adverse Effect”, “Knowledge”, “Company IP”, “Permitted Liens”
- Closing conditions and deliverables:
  - timing mechanics, escrow terms, payoff letters
- Reps and warranties:
  - fundamental reps vs business reps
  - materiality scrape
  - sandbagging / anti-sandbagging
  - bring-down standards
- Disclosure schedules:
  - properly referenced, consistent with reps
  - exception structure clear
- Covenants:
  - pre-closing conduct
  - access and cooperation
  - no shop / exclusivity (if any)
- Indemnification:
  - baskets, caps, survival
  - special indemnities
  - procedures
- Remedies:
  - specific performance
  - termination fee/break fee (if any)
- Employment / retention (if relevant)
- Tax matters:
  - elections, allocation, transfer taxes
- Post-closing covenants:
  - transition services, further assurances

---

## Employment / advisor agreements

- Scope and duties
- Compensation and benefits
- At-will vs term
- IP assignment (inventions assignment)
- Confidentiality + trade secrets
- Non-solicit / non-compete (jurisdiction-sensitive)
- Return of property
- Termination provisions and severance
- Dispute resolution / arbitration (jurisdiction-sensitive)

---

## NDAs

- Mutual vs one-way
- Definition of confidential info (include oral disclosures?)
- Use restrictions
- Term and survival
- Residuals clause (if any) and whether acceptable
- Injunctive relief language
- Compelled disclosure process

---

## Output tip

When you find a missing item, write it as:

- **Missing**: [provision]
- **Why it matters**:
- **Proposed clause** (short form):
- **Fallback**:
